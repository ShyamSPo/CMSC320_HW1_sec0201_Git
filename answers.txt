
Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Git has many utilities including allowing us to collaborate with others, manage our progress over time and display our finished (or even unfinished) projects for others to help with or see. 
It keeps all ur data and makes it easy to branch, merge or see a history of all changes made to a project. 


Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Data Science could be used in healthcare to get patient data or predict patient outcomes using prior medical data of past patients.
It could help with medical imaging analysis by displaying charts and it can even help with personal medicine plans.
